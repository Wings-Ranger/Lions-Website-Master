Community Projects

The Harcourt Lions Club is proud to support a range of local and national community initiatives. Details will be updated as projects progress.

Food Bank Support

Active

Regular donations and volunteer hours supporting the local food bank to help families in need across the region.

Lions Youth Camp

Upcoming

Annual camp for local youth to develop leadership skills, build friendships, and engage with their community.

Harcourt Beautification

Active

Ongoing roadside and park clean-ups, garden maintenance, and planting projects to keep Harcourt looking its best.

School Bursary Program

Active

Annual bursaries awarded to local students to assist with educational materials, uniforms, and school fees.

Emergency Relief Fund

Active

Financial and practical assistance for community members affected by bushfire, flood, or other emergencies.

Lions Eyeglass Recycling

Completed

Collection and distribution of recycled eyeglasses to people in developing countries through the Lions Eyeglass Recycling Program.
