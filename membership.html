Membership

Membership Requirements

  • Membership is open to all people of good character who are at least 18 years of age.
  • Members must be committed to the Lions Club mission of serving communities and making a positive impact.
  • Members are expected to actively participate in club activities, attend meetings, and contribute to service projects.
  • Membership dues must be paid annually to support operations and charitable initiatives.
  • Members should uphold integrity, respect, and compassion in all club and community interactions.
  • To become a member, express your interest to a current member or contact the club directly.
  • Prospective members may be invited to attend meetings or service projects to learn about club activities and values.
  • Membership applications may require sponsorship from a current member and committee approval.
  • Once accepted, new members are welcomed and encouraged to join service projects and initiatives.

Steps to Become a Member

  1. Express interest to a current member or contact the club directly.
  2. Attend a meeting or service project to learn more about the club.
  3. Submit a membership application with the required sponsorship.
  4. Wait for approval from the membership committee.
  5. Once accepted, participate in orientation and begin contributing to service projects.
